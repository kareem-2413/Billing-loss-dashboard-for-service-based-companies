Employees Billing Loss & AI Insights Dashboard

A Power BI analytics solution designed for service-based organizations to identify revenue leakage, analyze unbilled hours, and leverage AI-driven insights for faster decision-making. This project enables leadership teams to understand where, why, and how much billing loss is occurring, and provides predictive intelligence for future planning.

⭐ Problem Statement

Service companies routinely lose revenue when employees work hours that never get billed to clients.
This leakage occurs due to:

Idle or unproductive time

Timesheet entry errors

Internal meetings and non-billable activities

Delayed client approvals

Lack of visibility across teams and projects

Managers face two challenges:

They cannot clearly identify which employees, departments, or projects are responsible for unbilled hours.

Manual reporting consumes time and fails to provide predictive insights for upcoming risk.

The absence of a centralized analytics view leads to delayed decisions and sustained revenue loss.

⭐ Solution Overview

This project delivers an end-to-end Power BI dashboard that consolidates all billing, timesheet, and revenue loss metrics into a single intelligent workspace.
